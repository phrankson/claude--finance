## Tax Constants

| Constant | German Name | 2026 Value | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Basic tax-free allowance (single) | Grundfreibetrag | €12,096 | Below this: no Einkommensteuer |
| Basic tax-free allowance (married) | Grundfreibetrag | €24,192 | Zusammenveranlagung |
| Tax-free investment income (single) | Sparerpauschbetrag | €1,000 | Set via Freistellungsauftrag at broker |
| Tax-free investment income (married) | Sparerpauschbetrag | €2,000 | Combined across all brokers |
| Capital gains tax rate (flat) | Kapitalertragsteuer | 25% | Applies to dividends, interest, ETF gains |
| Solidarity surcharge on capital tax | Soli auf KapSt | 5.5% of 25% | Only applies above ~€18k additional income for most |
| Effective capital gains tax rate | Abgeltungsteuer | ~26.375% | KapSt 25% + Soli 5.5% × 25% |
| Church tax on capital gains | Kirchensteuer auf KapSt | 8% or 9% of KapSt | Bavaria/BW: 8%; others: 9%; only if Kirchenmitglied |
| Partial exemption — equity ETFs | Teilfreistellung | 30% | 30% of ETF gains exempt from tax |
| Annual ETF notional tax trigger | Vorabpauschale | Basiszins × 0.7 × fund value | Broker calculates and withholds automatically |
| Top income tax rate | Spitzensteuersatz | 42% (45% Reichensteuer above ~€277k) | Progressive: 14%→42% |

## Social Insurance (Sozialversicherung)

Rates are split 50/50 employer/employee unless noted.

| Insurance | German Name | Total Rate | Employee Share | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Health insurance (statutory) | GKV Beitragssatz | ~16.3% total | ~8.15% | 14.6% base + ~1.7% avg Zusatzbeitrag |
| Nursing care insurance (no children) | Pflegeversicherung | 3.4% | 1.7% | Extra 0.35% for childless above age 23 |
| Nursing care insurance (with children) | Pflegeversicherung | 3.05% | 1.525% | — |
| Pension insurance | Rentenversicherung | 18.6% | 9.3% | Mandatory for Angestellte |
| Unemployment insurance | Arbeitslosenversicherung | 2.6% | 1.3% | — |
| **Total SV employee burden (approx.)** | — | — | **~21%** | Varies with GKV Zusatzbeitrag |

**Beitragsbemessungsgrenze (BBG) West 2026:** €96,600/year (€8,050/month) — SV contributions capped above this income.

**Krankenversicherung threshold (JAEG):** €73,800/year gross — must exceed this to be eligible for PKV.

**GKV minimum contribution base (freiwillig versichert):** €1,178/month — floor for voluntarily insured (e.g., early retirees, freelancers). Minimum GKV contribution: ~€193/month (16.3% × €1,178).

## German Financial Glossary (for use in skill outputs)

| German Term | English Explanation |
|---|---|
| Rentenversicherung | Statutory pension insurance (mandatory for Angestellte) |
| Entgeltpunkte | Pension credit points (1.0 = average earner for that year) |
| Betriebliche Altersvorsorge (bAV) | Employer-sponsored occupational pension |
| Direktversicherung | Most common bAV form — life insurance policy via employer |
| Riester-Rente | State-subsidized private pension for Angestellte |
| Rürup-Rente (Basisrente) | Private pension primarily for self-employed / high earners |
| Krankenversicherung | Health insurance |
| GKV (gesetzliche Krankenversicherung) | Statutory health insurance (AOK, TK, Barmer, etc.) |
| PKV (private Krankenversicherung) | Private health insurance |
| JAEG (Jahresarbeitsentgeltgrenze) | Annual income threshold above which PKV is eligible |
| Kapitalertragsteuer | Flat 25% capital gains / investment income tax |
| Abgeltungsteuer | The full capital gains tax regime (KapSt + Soli) |
| Sparerpauschbetrag | Annual tax-free investment income allowance |
| Grundfreibetrag | Annual income tax-free allowance |
| Steuerklasse | Tax withholding class (I–VI; affects Lohnsteuer deduction) |
| Freistellungsauftrag | Standing order to broker to apply Sparerpauschbetrag |
| Vorabpauschale | Annual notional ETF tax on unrealized gains (broker handles) |
| Teilfreistellung | 30% exemption on equity ETF gains |
| Günstigerprüfung | IRS check: if marginal rate < 25%, capital income taxed at lower rate |
| ELSTER | German online tax portal (elster.de) |
| Anlage KAP | Tax form annex for capital income |
| Anlage Vorsorgeaufwand | Tax form annex for pension contributions |
| SCHUFA | German credit bureau |
| Tagesgeld | Instant-access savings account (like a HYSA) |
| Festgeld | Fixed-term deposit |
| Depot | Investment brokerage account |
| Dispositionskredit (Dispo) | Bank account overdraft facility |
| Ratenkredit | Consumer instalment loan |
| Baufinanzierung | Mortgage / real estate financing |
| Grunderwerbsteuer | Real estate transfer tax (paid at purchase) |
| Kaufnebenkosten | Total transaction costs on a property purchase |
| Schuldendienstquote | Debt service ratio (monthly debt payments ÷ net income) |
| Rentenlücke | Retirement income gap (target income minus projected pension) |
| Notgroschen | Emergency fund |
| Pflichtversicherung der Rentner (KVdR) | Statutory GKV coverage in retirement (income-tested) |
| Berufsunfähigkeitsversicherung (BU) | Occupational disability insurance |
| Haftpflichtversicherung | Personal liability insurance |
| Rechtsschutzversicherung | Legal expenses insurance |
| Hausratversicherung | Contents / household goods insurance |
